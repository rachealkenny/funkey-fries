Free Image Sources & Suggested Queries

Use these free image libraries and the sample queries below to source high-quality, royalty-free photos and illustrations for Funkey Fries.

- Unsplash (https://unsplash.com) — search: "fries", "fast food", "food closeup", "french fries". Good for vibrant food photography.
- Pexels (https://pexels.com) — search: "fries", "burger and fries", "food top view". Includes many free images and vectors.
- Pixabay (https://pixabay.com) — search: "french fries vector", "fast food illustration" for free SVGs and photos.
- Openverse (https://wordpress.org/openverse) — search for openly licensed images.

Tips
- Prefer landscape images ~16:9 for hero banners and square/cropped images for product cards.
- For illustrations, search "food illustration", "cartoon fries", or "flat food vector".
- Verify license on download (Unsplash/Pexels are free for commercial use; Pixabay may vary). Keep attribution if required.

Suggested local placeholders I added to `funkey-fries-web/public/` are simple SVGs you can replace with downloaded files.
